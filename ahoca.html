What is AHOCA Asia?

AHOCA Asia is a dynamic platform that unites visionary leaders in hospitality,business, and social impact across the Asia region. As the first regional branchof the America Hotel Owners Charitable Association, (AHOCA) we serve as a catalystfor collaboration, cultural exchange, and industry advancement. With strong support from our U.S. headquarters, we foster knowledge sharing betweenEast and West, promote sustainable business growth, and champion initiatives that benefit communities. Our network also emphasizes family governance as acornerstone for long-term success and responsible leadership.

Vision

To be Asia’s most influential hospitality and leadership network—connecting global perspectives, inspiring innovation, and driving sustainable growth. We envision a future where collaboration between East and West transforms the hospitality industry, strengthens family governance, and creates lasting positive impact for businesses and communities worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge East and West by creating a dynamic platform for hospitality leaders, entrepreneurs, and business innovators to collaborate, share knowledge, and lead impactful community initiatives. With the support of our U.S. headquarters, we facilitate cultural exchange, promote sustainable business growth, and uphold family governance principles — empowering members to contribute to industry development and societal well-being.

Why join us?

Joining our association means becoming part of a vibrant, forward-thinking community that bridges East and West, connecting visionary business leaders across borders. We provide a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange, strategic insights, and collaborative opportunities that empower members to thrive in an increasingly interconnected global economy. Whether you seek to expand your network, gain actionable intelligence, or unlock new business partnerships, our association is your gateway to meaningful growth and innovation.
